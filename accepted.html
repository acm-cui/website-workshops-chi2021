Let's Talk About CUIs: Putting Conversational User Interface Design Into Practice

Workshop at the 2021 ACM CHI Conference

Accepted Papers

Below are the accepted papers for this workshop:

  • Why should we care about register? Reflections on chatbot language design: Ana Paula Chaves, Marco Aurelio Gerosa
  • Conversational User Interfaces As Assistive interlocutors For Young Children’s Bilingual Language Acquisition: Neelma Bhatti, Timothy L. Stelter, D. Scott McCrickard
  • Effects of Gendered Voices on Personality Perceptions of Conversational User Interfaces: Irene Lopatovska, Elena Korshakova, Diedre Brown
  • Exploring preferences on chatbot’s guidance type and timing: Su Fang Yeh, Meng-Hsin Wu, Yung-Ju Chang
  • What Would Be the Principles for Successful Trollbot Design?: Heidi Vepsäläinen, Antti Salovaara, Henna Paakki
  • In (Private, Secure & Conversational) FinBots We Trust: Magdalene Ng, Kovila P.L. Coopamootoo, Dave Horsfall, Ehsan Toreini, Aad van Moorsel, Tasos Spiliotopoulos, Mhairi Aitken,Karen Elliott
  • When Everyday Things Speak to Us: Anuradha Reddy, A. Baki Kocaballi, Iohanna Nicenboim, Marie Louise Juul Søndergaard, Maria Luce Lupetti, et al. (17 authors total)
  • CUIs for older adults: Kathrin Koebel, Martin Lacayo, Madhumitha Murali, Ioannis Tarnanas, Arzu Çöltekin
  • Designing Credible Conversational Voice Interfaces for Older Adults: Alisha Pradhan, Amanda Laza
  • How Do User Moods Affect Perceived Delays in Crowd-Powered Conversational Interactions?: Tahir Abbas, Ujwal Gadiraju, Panos Markopoulos
  • Without Being Asked: Identifying Use-Cases for Explanations in Interaction with Voice Assistants: Dominik Pins, Fatemeh Alizadeh
  • Methods for Co-Designing Conversational Interactions for Digital Food Journaling: Lucas M. Silva, David V. Nguyen, Daniel A. Epstein
  • Heuristic Evaluation of Conversational Agents: Raina Langevin, Ross Lordon, Thi Avrahami, Benjamin Cowan, Tad Hirsch, Gary Hsieh
  • Designing a Conversational Agent for Pragmatics Learning: Liquan Chai, Dave Murray-Rust, Maria K. Wolters
  • Co-designing CUIs to Support the Use and Transmission of Indigenous Languages: Jennyfer Lawrence Taylor
  • In the Hours of Transition from Web to Conversational Search: Sruthi Viswanathan, Fabien Guillot, Antonietta Maria Grasso, Jean-Michel Renders
  • Toward Privacy Implications in Developing and Deploying Chatbots: Analysis of Chatbot Development Platforms: Diva Smriti, Rahil Rathod, Jina Huh-Yoo
  • Context-Specific Usability Measures for Voice Assistants: Sanjana Mendu, Shyam S. Sundar, Saeed Abdullah
