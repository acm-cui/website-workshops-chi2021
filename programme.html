Let's Talk About CUIs: Putting Conversational User Interface Design Into Practice

Workshop at the 2021 ACM CHI Conference

Programme

The workshop will last for 4hrs, from May 8th, 22:00 JST - May 9th, 02:00 JST (please click here for timezone conversion). All times on the program are in the host timezone (JST- Japan Standard Time).

The general program will be as follows:

  • 22:00 - 22:45 : Organizer and Participant Introduction Presentations
  • 22:45 - 23:45 : Breakout Session 1: What problems exist in CUI Design?
    • 22:45 - 23:30: Small Group Brainstorm
    • 23:30 - 23:45: Large Group Review
  • 23:45 - 00:45: Breakout Session 2: Where does the academia-industry gap for CUI design sit?
    • 23:45 - 00:30: Small Group Brainstorm
    • 00:30 - 00:45: Large Group Review
  • 00:45 - 01:00: Break
  • 01:00 - 01:45: Final Session: What are actionable steps we can take to bridge the academia-industry gap and improve CUI design?
  • 01:45 - 02:00: Closing Comments
